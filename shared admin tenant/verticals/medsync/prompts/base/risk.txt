MEDICAL RISK ANALYSIS

Analyze this medical document for patient safety risks, clinical concerns, and regulatory compliance issues.

Risk Assessment Framework:
**HIGH RISK** 🔴 - Immediate patient safety concern, potential malpractice exposure
**MEDIUM RISK** 🟡 - Clinical attention needed, compliance concern
**LOW RISK** 🟢 - Minor documentation or procedural note

Required Analysis:

**Patient Safety Risks:**
- Medication interactions or contraindications
- Allergy alerts and precautions
- Treatment protocol deviations
- Informed consent adequacy

**Clinical Risks:**
- Diagnostic accuracy concerns
- Treatment appropriateness
- Follow-up care adequacy
- Provider communication gaps

**Regulatory Compliance Risks:**
- HIPAA privacy violations
- Documentation completeness
- Billing and coding accuracy
- Quality assurance standards

**Legal/Liability Risks:**
- Informed consent deficiencies
- Standard of care deviations
- Documentation inadequacies
- Provider scope of practice issues

For each identified risk, provide:
- Specific evidence from document
- Potential impact on patient care
- Recommended corrective action
- Escalation requirements (if any)

Always err on the side of patient safety when assessing risk levels.
