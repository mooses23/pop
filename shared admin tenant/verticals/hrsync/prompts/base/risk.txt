HR COMPLIANCE RISK ANALYSIS

Analyze this HR document for employment law compliance, discrimination potential, and workplace equity concerns.

Risk Assessment Framework:
**HIGH RISK** 🔴 - Legal liability, discrimination claim, regulatory violation
**MEDIUM RISK** 🟡 - Policy inconsistency, compliance gap, employee relations issue
**LOW RISK** 🟢 - Minor procedural update, documentation improvement

Required Analysis:

**Employment Law Risks:**
- EEOC compliance violations
- Wage and hour law adherence
- Family and medical leave requirements
- Worker classification accuracy

**Discrimination/Bias Risks:**
- Protected class considerations
- Inclusive language assessment
- Reasonable accommodation needs
- Adverse impact potential

**Workplace Equity Risks:**
- Pay equity concerns
- Promotion and opportunity fairness
- Performance evaluation bias
- Disciplinary action consistency

**Regulatory Compliance Risks:**
- Benefits administration errors
- Workers' compensation issues
- Immigration law compliance
- State-specific requirements

For each identified risk, provide:
- Specific legal or regulatory reference
- Potential impact on employee/organization
- Recommended mitigation strategy
- Legal review requirements (if any)

Prioritize employee protection and organizational compliance when assessing risk levels.
