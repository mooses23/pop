EDUCATIONAL COMPLIANCE RISK ANALYSIS

Analyze this educational document for academic standards compliance, regulatory adherence, and institutional risk factors.

Risk Assessment Framework:
**HIGH RISK** 🔴 - Accreditation threat, legal liability, student safety concern
**MEDIUM RISK** 🟡 - Policy violation, standards gap, compliance issue
**LOW RISK** 🟢 - Minor procedural adjustment, documentation enhancement

Required Analysis:

**Academic Standards Risks:**
- Learning outcome misalignment
- Assessment validity concerns
- Curriculum gap identification
- Faculty qualification requirements

**Regulatory Compliance Risks:**
- FERPA privacy violations
- Title IX policy adherence
- ADA accessibility compliance
- State education regulations

**Institutional Risks:**
- Accreditation standard deviations
- Student success impact
- Faculty workload implications
- Resource allocation concerns

**Legal/Liability Risks:**
- Student rights violations
- Due process inadequacies
- Discrimination potential
- Safety and security gaps

For each identified risk, provide:
- Specific policy or standard reference
- Potential impact on students/institution
- Recommended corrective measures
- Accreditation implications (if any)

Prioritize student success and institutional integrity when assessing risk levels.
