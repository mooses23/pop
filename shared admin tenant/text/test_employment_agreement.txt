EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made between TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and Jane Smith ("Employee"), effective January 1, 2025.

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer and shall perform duties as assigned by the Company's management team.

2. COMPENSATION
Company shall pay Employee a base salary of $120,000 annually, payable in accordance with Company's standard payroll practices.

3. BENEFITS
Employee shall be eligible for health insurance, dental coverage, and 401(k) participation after 90 days of employment.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information and trade secrets of the Company during and after employment.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Company may terminate immediately for cause.

6. NON-COMPETE
Employee agrees not to compete with Company's business for 12 months after termination within a 50-mile radius.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
